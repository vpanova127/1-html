    • Mirko works with clients from cultural establishments, luxury fashion houses, to commercial entities, providing a wide range of design services. Over the years the Bureau Borsche has established a longterm partnership, producing full service graphic design, with The Bavarian State Opera, Bavarian Radio Symphony Orchestra, and The Design Museum. The editorial projects are at the core and include over ten years of continued creative direction for Zeit Magazin and Spike art publications. The Bureau has worked on global campaigns for both Nike and Apple. Since 2020, Mirko has been recruited by Supreme NY to work on seasonal graphics and creative services. The Bureau’s branding commissions include Balenciaga, Givenchy, Rimowa, Farfetch, Highsnobiety, Zara Athleticz and in 2021 they were able to fulfil a dream by working with one of the biggest football clubs in Europe, Internazionale Milano, for their new visual identity.
